Mezcal Creyente

Art Direction.

2017 - 2020 / Agency: The Media Grind / Role: Junior Art Director

ART DIRECTION

I curated monthly social calendars for Mezcal Creyente using a mix of content sources. I regularly color-corrected and Photoshopped images to ensure visual cohesiveness. For agency run photoshoots, I assisted the Senior Art Director through all phases of production, from concepting ideas to sourcing props, post-production of the assets and exporting them for social media.


GLACCE CAMPAIGN

In 2019, Mezcal Creyente partnered with a popular crystal water bottle brand, Glacce, to create a customized mixology kit featuring special crystals.

I concepted and created mock-ups of the mixology pieces that were eventually produced for the kit: one glass tumbler, one stir rod, and two crystal drinking glasses. I collaborated with the Senior Art Director to produce a photoshoot where I served as the cocktail stylist manager.
