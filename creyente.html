Mezcal Creyente

Art Direction.

2017 - 2020 / Agency: The Media Grind / Role: Junior Art Director

ART DIRECTION

Every month, I curated the social calendar for Mezcal Creyente using a mix of content sources. To ensure that all content matched the brand look and feel, I regularly color-corrected and Photoshopped images accordingly. For agency run photoshoots, I collaborated with the Senior Art Director through all phases of production, from concepting ideas to sourcing props, to post-production of the assets and finally exporting them for social media.


GLACCE CAMPAIGN

In 2019, Mezcal Creyente partnered with a popular crystal water bottle brand, Glacce, to create a customized mixology kit featuring special crystals.

With my experience in cocktail creation, I concepted and created mock-ups of the mixology pieces that were eventually produced for the kit: one glass tumbler, one stir rod, and two crystal drinking glasses. To showcase these pieces, I collaborated with the Senior Art Director to produce a photoshoot where I served as the cocktail stylist manager.
