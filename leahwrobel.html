Brand Identity

In 2016, Leah and I began our mission to create a visual brand identity for her yoga practice. With Leah's feedback, I designed the logo, established the brand color palette, and created the brand look and feel. To bring this visual look to life, I designed new business cards, gift certificates, and flyers.

WEB DESIGN

As part of the rebranding process, I redesigned the brand website using Weebly. I customized the HTML/CSS code of an existing template to maintain a consistent brand look and feel. I collaborated with Leah to organize the website pages to optimize user-friendliness and direct user traffic. See for yourself - it's easy to navigate and easy on the eyes!
