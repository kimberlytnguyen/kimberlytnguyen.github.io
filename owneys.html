Owney's Rum

Art Direction. Retouching.

2017 - 2018 / Agency: The Media Grind / Role: Junior Art Director

ART DIRECTION

Every month, I curated the social calendar for Owney's Rum using a mix of content sources. To ensure that all content matched the brand look and feel, I regularly color-corrected and Photoshopped images accordingly. For agency run photoshoots, I collaborated with the Senior Art Director through all phases of production, from concepting ideas to sourcing props, to cocktail styling (!) on set, to post-production of the assets and finally exporting them for social media.


And yes, I did style all the cocktails pictured above :)


RETOUCHING

Imagine this: liquid drippings everywhere, greasy fingerprints on glassware, citrus pulp flying all over the props. This is life on the set of a cocktail photoshoot. So how do we get these images looking pristine afterward? The answer: call me, your retouch magician!
