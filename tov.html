Three Olives Vodka

Art Direction. Cocktail Styling.

2017 - 2019 / Agency: The Media Grind / Role: Junior Art Director

ROSÉ ALL DAY & NIGHT

To promote the launch of the new Three Olives Rosé Vodka, I collaborated with the Senior Art Director to bring to life the creative for this campaign. The concept? Rosé all day... and night ;)

Together, we created all photo and video assets to amplify on social media, as well as print collateral to support the campaign offline. I worked with the Senior Art Director to bring the visions to life from start to finish. My main role was to assist with concept ideation, mock-up creation, source and style props, wardrobe, and cocktail style on set.


Monthly Editorial Calendars

To support Three Olives' entire flavor portfolio, we also created regular photo/video content to go live on social each month. As Junior Art Director, I assisted with concept ideation every month, creating mock-ups, sourcing the props/wardrobe, and cocktail styling on set. To aid with post-production, I worked my photoshop magic and regularly retouched photo assets.
