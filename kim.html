About Me

It all started when my brother taught me how to Photoshop my 9-year-old face onto the body of an orangutan.

All my life, I've been drawn to making cool stuff digitally. As an early teen, I self-taught myself how to Photoshop by creating fun images of my dog riding rainbows. Soon after, I got my first DSLR camera and discovered my natural eye for composition by taking pictures of my mom's flower garden. In high school, I was all about video creation and tried to make it big on YouTube with my family's tape camcorder. In college, graphic design was my calling and I ran a successful side hustle as a top-rated seller creating collateral for small businesses. After college, I taught myself basic HTML/CSS and learned to make this website.

When I'm not working, you can find me tending to my plant children, learning dance choreography in my living room, or crafting a tasty dish in the kitchen.
