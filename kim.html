About Me

It all started when my brother taught me how to Photoshop my nine-year-old face onto the body of a baby orangutan.

Throughout the years, I've dabbled in various creative adventures. Photoshop was my jam in middle school, and I was obsessed with special effects tutorials, like making my dog ride a rainbow into outer space. Soon after, I picked up my first DSLR camera and found my love for photography by capturing an epic pic of a housefly pooping on my mom's roses. In high school, I was all about video creation, tried to make it big on YouTube, and created fun video projects with my family's tape camcorder. In college, I experimented with graphic design and ran a successful side hustle creating flyers for small businesses. After college, I taught myself basic HTML/CSS and learned to make this website. My next calling? UX Design!

When I'm not working hard behind my laptop, you can find me tending to my plant children, learning dance choreography, or whipping up a tasty dish in the kitchen.
